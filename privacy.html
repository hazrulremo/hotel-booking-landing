Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Hotel Information: Name, address, contact details
  • Customer Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Booking Information: Room details, dates, amounts
  • Payment Information: Transaction records
  • User Account Information: Username, role, display name

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage bookings and room availability
  • To send booking confirmations and reminders
  • To process payments and track financial records
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To communicate important updates and notifications

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure Google Sheets servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing our hotel management services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our dashboard.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Google Sheets, Google Apps Script, and email services. These services have their own privacy policies that you should review.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the dashboard.
